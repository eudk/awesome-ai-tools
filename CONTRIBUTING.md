# Contributing to Awesome AI Tools

Thank you for your interest in contributing! We welcome suggestions that improve the quality of this list. Before submitting, please take a moment to review the guidelines below.

---

## Guiding Principles

To maintain the quality and focus of this list, contributions should align with the following:

1. **High Quality & Established**  
   Tools should be public, functional, and usable right now.  
   We generally do not include:
   - waitlists or "coming soon" pages  
   - placeholder demos or landing pages without a real product  

2. **Real Value Over Hype**  
   Tools should provide clear, practical value. Submissions that are overly generic, duplicated, or primarily promotional may be declined.

3. **Relevance**  
   This list focuses on tools for development, creativity, productivity, and learning.

*Maintainers reserve the right to make the final judgment on whether a submission fits the scope and quality of the project.*

---

## How to Add a Tool or Resource

1. **Ensure it's a good fit**  
   Please only suggest tools that align with the principles above.

2. **Check for duplicates**  
   Use `Ctrl+F` (or `Cmd+F`) to make sure the tool isn't already listed.

3. **Add to the correct section**

4. **Follow the format**

[Tool Name](Official URL) - A brief, one-line description.


5. **Create a Pull Request**  
Include a short explanation of what the tool does and why it should be added.

---

## Submission Notes

- Tools must be **publicly accessible and usable at the time of submission**
- Submissions should be **concise and factual** (avoid marketing language)
- If you have a connection to the tool (creator, contributor, etc.), please mention it in your PR

Submissions that are incomplete, duplicated, or primarily promotional may be closed.

---

## Transparency (Optional)

If AI tools or automated agents were used to prepare the submission, you may mention it briefly. Clear and honest submissions help keep the list high quality.

---

## Reporting Broken Links or Outdated Information

If you find a dead link or outdated information, please [open an issue](https://github.com/eudk/awesome-ai-tools/issues).

---

Thank you for helping keep this list useful and up to date.
