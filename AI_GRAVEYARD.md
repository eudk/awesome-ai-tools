# 🪦 AI Graveyard

A running list of AI tools, products, models, and experiments that were discontinued, shut down, absorbed, renamed out of existence, or retired.

The AI industry evolves quickly, and many products or features are replaced, rebranded, or discontinued within a short time.

---

## Dead AI products, projects, and features

- **Sora** — OpenAI shut down the standalone AI video app in March 2026 after a limited public phase.

- **Google Bard** — The Bard product name was discontinued and replaced by **Gemini**.

- **Operator (standalone site)** — Folded into ChatGPT agent, ending the standalone product.

- **Cortana** — Gradually phased out across Windows and Microsoft 365 products.

- **Cortana on Harman Kardon Invoke** — Support for Cortana integration on the Invoke speaker was discontinued.

- **Humane AI Pin** — Sales were stopped, assets were partially acquired, and cloud-dependent functionality was discontinued.

- **Character.AI old/beta site** — Retired in 2024 while the main platform continued.

- **Meta celebrity lookalike AI chatbots** — Discontinued as part of changes to Meta’s AI product strategy.

- **Meta AI bot profiles** — Removed as part of changes to Meta’s AI product offerings.

- **Google Duplex on the Web** — The web automation version of Duplex was discontinued.

- **Google AI Test Kitchen app** — The mobile app was removed as Google consolidated its AI experiments.

- **Midjourney Rooms** — Discontinued in 2026 as part of feature changes.

- **ChatGPT Plugins** — Retired in April 2024 and replaced by Custom GPTs.

- **Bing Chat (brand)** — Rebranded and integrated into **Microsoft Copilot**.

- **Google Duet AI (brand)** — Rebranded to **Gemini for Google Workspace**.

- **Inflection AI (Pi – original vision)** — Company direction shifted after key team members moved to Microsoft, ending the original consumer-focused strategy.

- **Artifact** — Shut down as a standalone product and partially integrated into Yahoo.

- **DeepMind Sparrow** — Announced but never released publicly; development efforts were incorporated into later systems.

- **Amazon Alexa LLM upgrade (early versions)** — Early iterations were replaced as Amazon revised its approach.

- **Replika (original AI companion experience)** — The original feature set was significantly restricted, changing the product experience.

- **YouChat (early versions)** — Early implementations were replaced during multiple product iterations.

- **Kuki / Mitsuku** — Legacy chatbot system later displaced by newer LLM-based systems.

- **Cleverbot** — Early conversational AI system later displaced by newer models.
